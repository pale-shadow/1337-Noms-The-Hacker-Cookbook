# @Gr4vy's Corn Mac n Cheese

Best side dish ever

- Category: SIDES
- Credit: @Gr4vy
- Yield: 4
- Prep time: 15 minutes
- Cook time: 1 hour
- Difficulty: Very very easy

## Ingredients

- 1 can (15 oz) corn
- 1 can (14.75 oz) cream corn
- 1 cup macaroni
- 1 stick butter
- 1/2 lb Velveeta

## Instructions

1. Dump everything into a baking dish. Do not drain corn. You can cut the cheese into small pieces. Bake at 350°F for an hour, stirring once.

## Notes

Scales up nicely.  If it's too thick,  add a little milk.   If it's too thin,  bake a little longer.

## Dietary Notes

Vegetarian
