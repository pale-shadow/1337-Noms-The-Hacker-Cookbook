# jcase's LowCarb Alabama White BBQ Sauce Recipe

Goes best on chicken. Cook chicken on a smoker, dip in sauce. Goes great
on wings.

## Ingredients

- 1 Cup Mayonnaise
- 1 Tbps Ground Black Pepper
- 1 Tbps Salt
- 3 Tbps Lemon Juice
- 3 Tbps White Vinegar
- 2 Tbps splenda

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients in blender
2. Blend well
